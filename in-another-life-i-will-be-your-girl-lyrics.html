18. she will be a polite girl in the partyubah jadi (S.Ps):

Subject : English

Category : Tenses

Sub-category : Past Simple

Level : ES, JHS

Code : 5

Past simple tense is used when the action starts and ends in the past.

Structure with infinitives:  

(+) S + preterite (v₂)

(-) S + did not + infinitive

(?) Did + S + infinitive


Structure with be :

(+) S + was/ were + complement

(-) S + was/ were + not + complement

(?) was/ were + S + complement

In the sentences that use be instead of infinitive, 'was' is used for the subjects 'I' and the third person singular. 'were' is used for the subject 'you' and plural.

Time signal :  yesterday, just now, ... ago, last ..., etc.


ANSWER :  

Simple Future → She will be a polite girl in the party.

Past Simple → She was a polite girl in the party.
