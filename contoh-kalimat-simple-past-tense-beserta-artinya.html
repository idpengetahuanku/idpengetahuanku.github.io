4. Apakah simple past tense dengan past tense sama? Dan berikan rumus serta contoh kalimatnya

Rumus Active voice :

Positive form : Subject + The second form of the verb + Object + Time Signal

Contoh : John kicked the ball last game

Negative form : Subject + didn't + verb + object + Time Signal

Contoh : Bob didn't eat dinner last Wednesday

Interrogative form : Did + Subject + verb + object + Time Signal + ?

Contoh : Did you report the problem yesterday?

Rumus Passive voice :

Positive form : Subject + was / were + the third form of the verb / adverb + Time signal

Contoh : You were graduated last year

Negative form : Subject + wasn't / weren't + the third form of the verb / adverb + time signal

Contoh : Mary wasn't happy just now

Interrogative form : Was / were + Subject + the third form of the verb / adverb + time signal + ?

Contoh : Was it the book yesterday?

18. Tulis rumus simple past tense dan simple present tense Beserta Contohnya


Simple past tense:

subject + V2 + O

ex:
(+) I went to primary school in England.
(-) My grandmother didn't like cats.
(?) Did you get my letter?


Simple present tense:

Subject + V(use s at the back if the subject are he, she, it) + O

ex:
(+) She wears jeans all the time.
(-) He doesn't enjoy it.
(?) Does Jack smoke?@Answer:

1) Simple Present
(+) S + V1(s/es) + O/C => She reads a book.
(-) S + do/does + not + V1 + O/C => She doesn't read a book.
(?) Do/does + S + V1 + O/C? => Does she read a book?

2) Simple Past
(+) S + V2 + O/C => We cook a cake.
(-) S + did + not + V1 + O/C => We didn't cook a cake.
(?) Did + S + V1 + O/C? => Did we cook a cake?
