Jawaban dicetak tebal dan merupakan materi pembelajaran bentuk kalimat Active dan Passive Voice dalam Bahasa Inggris.

Perlu diketahui bahwa terdapat 5 Non Convertible Tenses, artinya terdapat 5 bentuk kalimat yang tidak bisa dirubah ke dalam bentuk Passive Voice:

Present Perfect Continuous TensePast Perfect Continuous TenseFuture Perfect Continuous TenseFuture Continuous TenseFuture Perfect Tense

Kalimat aktif subjeknya aktif melakukan aksi dalam kalimat.

Kalimat pasif subjeknya pasif dikenai aksi dalam kalimat.

Active Voice:

1. Simple Present Tense:

(+) Subject + Verb-1 S/ES + Object

2. Present Continuous Tense:

(+) Subject + Tobe (is/am/are) + Verb-ing + Object

3. Present Perfect Tense:

(+) Subject + Have/Has + Verb-3 + Object

4. Simple Past Tense:

(+) Subject + Verb-2 + Object

5. Past Continuous Tense:

(+) Subject + Tobe (was/were) + Verb-ing + Object

6. Past Perfect Tense:

(+) Subject + Had + Verb-3 + Object

7. Simple Future Tense:

(+) Subject + Will + Verb-1 + Object

8. Future Continuous Tense:

(+) Subject + Will + Be + Verb-ing + Object

9. Future Perfect Tense:

(+) Subject + Will + Have + Verb-3 + Object

Passive Voice:

1. Passive Voice [Simple Present Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + to be (is/am/are) + Verb3

2. Passive Voice [Present Continuous Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + to be (is/am/are) + being + Verb3

3. Passive Voice [Present Perfect Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + have/has + been + Verb3

4. Passive Voice [Simple Past Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + to be (was – were) + Verb 3

5. Passive Voice [Past Continuous Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + to be (was – were) + Being + Verb3

6. Passive Voice [Past Perfect Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + had + been + Verb3

7. Passive Voice [Simple Future Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + will + be + Verb3

3. Apakah future continuous tense tidak dapat diubah ke dalam bentuk passive voice?


Aturan dalam kalimat passive : Subject dalam kalimat aktif menjadi object dalam kalimat pasif Sebaliknya object dalam kalimat aktif menjadi subject dalam kalimat pasifKalimat yang dipasifkan harus memiliki object Jika kalimat actif tidak memiliki auxiliary (to be), maka kita harus menambahkan (to be) yang disesuaikan dengan subject yang digunakan dalam kalimat pasif juga harus disesuaikan dengan bentuk tenses yang dipasifkanLetakkan kata kerja utama (verb) setelah to be pada kalimat pasif yaitu kata kerja bentuk ke 3 Letakkan preposision “by” setelah verba utama dalam kalimat pasif sebelum objectSusunan kalimat atau pola yang dipasifkan harus sesuai dengan tensesVerb transitive tidak digunakan dalam kalimat pasif, kecuali kalimat tersebut menggunakan cognate object dalam aktifEx : she sang a fine song
A fine song was sung by her


Penggunaan “by”

1. Penggunaan passive voice tanpa “by+O”

a. Bila tidak menyebutkan pelaku atau tidak penting untuk mengetahui siapa yang melakukan pekerjaan

Example : rice is grown in Thailand

b. Manakala tidak tahu siapa pelaku dalam pekerjaan tersebut

Example : this house was built in 1981

c. Manakala subject dalam kalimat pasif (onject pelakunya)... people, someone, somebody

Example : somebody intoduce me to Dr. Felix last year

I was intoduced to Dr. Felix last year

2. Penggunaan passive voice dengan menggunakan “by+O”

a. Jika perlu mengetahui si pelaku baik person or thing

Example : life on Mussissipi was written by Mark Twain

b. Sebagai pengganti kalimat aktif ketika si pembicara ingin memfokuskan pada subject active voice dari pada object active voice

Example : this glove was made by my aunt

5. Write ( tulis ) 5 kalimat pasif yaitu : 2 passive voice present tense, 2 passive voice future tense and 1 passive voice present perfect tense.Tulis kalimat aktifnya terlebih dahulu kemudian buat kalimat pasif.


Penjelasan:

PEMBAHASAN

Kalimat pasif dapat dibentuk dari tiga tenses utama dalam bahasa Inggris, yaitu present tense, past tense dan future tense. Pada artikel ini akan dijelaskan mengenai rumus serta contoh yang digunakan dalam membentuk kalimat pasif pada simple future tense. Penjelasan akan terbagi menjadi tiga, yakni kalimat pasif dalam bentuk kalimat positif, kalimat negatif serta kalimat pertanyaan.

1. RUMUS DAN CONTOH

KALIMAT POSITIF

Rumus: Subjek + will/shall + be + Verb III.

Contoh:

I will give you a writing assignment in Japanese tomorrow. (Aktif)Security officer will ask to show our identification. (Aktif)

You will be given a writing assignment in Japanese tomorrow. (Pasif)

We will be asked to show our identification by security officer. (Pasif)

2.RUMUS DAN CONTOH

KALIMAT NEGATIF

Rumus: Subjek + will/shall + not + be + Verb III.

contoh:

He will meet them next Sunday at Tebet. (Aktif)The farmers will harvest the crops next month. (Aktif)

They will not be met by him next Sunday at Tebet. (Pasif)

The crops shall not be harvested by the farmers next month. (Pasif)

3.RUMUS DAN CONTOH

KALIMAT PERTANYAAN

Rumus: Will/shall + subjek + be + Verb III.

contoh:

They will buy a bike for her next week. (Aktif)The waitress will serve them. (Aktif)They will not be met by him next Sunday at Tebet. (Pasif)The crops shall not be harvested by the farmers next month. (Pasif)

11. Buatlah masing masing satu kalimat active voice dari1. present perfect continuous tense 2. past perfect continuous tense 3. future perfect tense 4. past future perfect tense 5. future perfect continuous tense 6. past future perfect continuous tense.lalu ubah kalimat ke passive voice ​


Jawaban:

1) Active and Passive Voice "Present Perfect Continuous Tense".

Rumus Active:

Subject + To be + Been + Verb(ing) + O/C.

Rumus Passive:

Subject + To be + Been + Being + V3.

Contoh kalimat:

Active Voice: Angelina has been practicing her English ability.

Passive Voice: Practicing English has been being ability by Angelina.

2) Active and Passive Voice "Past Perfect Continuous Tense".

Rumus Active:

Subject + To be + Been + Verb(ing) + O/C.

Rumus Passive:

Subject + To be + Been + Being + V3.

Contoh kalimat:

Active Voice: Sam was tired because he had been jogging.

Passive Voice: Jogging had been being tired by Sam.

3) Active and Passive Voice "Future Perfect Tense".

Rumus Active:

Subject + Will + Have+V3.

Rumus Passive:

Subject + Will + Have + Been + V3 + by...

Contoh kalimat:

Active Voice: She will have won the first prize.

Passive Voice: The first prize will have been won by her.

4) Active and Passive Voice "Past Future Perfect Tense".

Rumus Active:

Subject + Would/Should/Could/May/Might/ Must + Have + V3/Past Participile.

Rumus Passive:

Subject + Would + Have + Been + V3.

Contoh kalimat:

Active Voice:

I would have been eating dinner with my friends today. Angelina would have been gone to the salon after lunch. I would have been studying about martial arts.

Passive Voice:

Dinner would have been eaten today by my friends and meSalon would have been gone after lunch by Angelina.Martial arts would have been studied about by me.

5) Active and Passive Voice "Future Perfect Continuous Tense".

Rumus Active:

Subject + Will + To be + Been + Verb(ing) + O/C.

Subject + To be + Going to + To be + Been + Verb(ing) + O/C.

Rumus Passive:

Subject + Will + To be + Been + Being + V3.

Subject + To be + Going to + To be + Been + Being + V3.

Contoh kalimat:

Active Voice:

My friend will have been graduating from University of Indonesia by the end of this month.We will have been singing a few song at the party.I will have been listening a music tonight.Emily have been feeding the cat.

Passive Voice:

University of Indonesia will have been being graduated by the end of this month by my friend.A few song will have been being sung at the party by us. A music will have been being listened by me.The cat will have been being fed by Emily.

6) Active and Passive Voice "Past Future Perfect Continuous Tense".

Rumus Active:

Subject + Would + Have + Been + V1-ing/Present Participle.

Rumus Passive:

Subject + Would + Have + Been + Being + V3.

Contoh kalimat:

Active Voice:

I would have been driving the car.Angelina and her family would have been living at their new house if its construction had finished.We would have been leaving on vacation in Bali island.They would have been playing martial arts.I would have been sleeping to finish my homework.We would have been going to the cinemas tonight.She would have been eating health food because it is good for health.We would have been going to the museum.I would have been learning Japanese language.We would have been learning Thai language.We would have been going to martial arts.

Passive Voice:

The car would have been being driven by me.Their new house would have been being left if its construction had finished by Angelina and her family.Bali island would have been being left by us.Martial arts would have been being played by them.My homework would have been being slept by me.Health food would have been being eaten because it is good for health by her.The museum would have been being gone by us.Japanese language would have been being learnt by me.Thai language would have been being learnt by us.Martial arts would have been being gone by us.

Contoh:

Aktif : Students speak English

Pasif : English is spoken by students

Simple Past Tense

Aktif : S + V2 + O

Pasif : S + to be (was, were) + V3 + by + O

Contoh :

Aktif : Students spoke English

Pasif : English was spoken by students

Passive voice

1. simple present tense

S (direct object) + is/am/are + past participle +/- by … (agent)

2. simple past tense

S (direct object) + was/were + past participle +/- by … (agent)

3. simple future tense

S (direct object) +will + be + past participle +/- by … (agent)
