Pembahasan

Gengo「言語」

Artinya berarti bahasa, dalam bahasa Jepang ketika ingin menyatakan suatu bahasa dari suatu negara maka gunakan pola :

(nama negara) + akhiran 〜語 (go).

Contoh :

日本語 (nihongo) : bahasa Jepang.インドネシア語 (indoneshiago) : bahasa Indonesia.英語 (eigo) : bahasa Inggris.

----------------------

Mengenai keterangan waktu

Ji「時」

Dalam bahasa Indonesia artinya yaitu jam atau pukul, berikut ini kosakata mengenai jam dalam bahasa Jepang :

1時 (ichi ji) : jam 1.2時 (ni ji) : jam 2.3時 (san ji) : jam 3.4時 (yo ji) : jam 4.5時 (go ji) : jam 5.6時 (roku ji) : jam 6.7時 (shichi ji) : jam 7.8時 (hachi ji) : jam 8.9時 (ku ji) : jam 9.10時 (juu ji) : jam 10.11時 (juu ichi ji) : jam 11.12時 (juu ni ichi) : jam 12.

Gozen「 午前 • ごぜん 」

Untuk menyatakan pukul A.M, yang berarti waktu tengah malam-siang (00.00-11.59).

Gogo「 午後 • ごご 」

Untuk menyatakan pukul P.M, yang berarti dapat digunakan dalam waktu siang-tengah malam (12.00-23.59).

Fun/pun「 分 • ふん / ぷん 」

Untuk menunjukkan menit dalam bahasa Jepang, maka dari itu dari 分 (fun/pun) ialah menit.

Berikut ini kosakata menit :

1 分 (ip pun) : 1 menit.2 分 (ni fun) : 2 menit.3 分 (san pun) : 3 menit.4 分 (yon pun) : 4 menit.5 分 (go fun) : 5 menit.6 分 (rop pun) : 6 menit.7 分 (nana fun) : 7 menit.8 分 (hap pun) : 8 menit.9 分 (kyuu pun) : 9 menit.10 分 (juu pun) : 10 menit.20 分 (ni juu pun) : 20 menit.15 分 (juu go fun) : 15 menit.30 分 (san juu pun) : 30 menit.60 分 (roku juu pun) : 60 menit.

Di soal nomor 2, arti dari 午後2時15分 (Gogo ni ji juu go fun) artinya Jam 2:15 P.M.

Kemudian pada soal nomor 4, di soal tersebut menanyakan mengenai pernyataan yang salah mengenai menit dalam bahasa Jepang, namun ada 2 opsi yang termasuk salah yaitu :

A. 10 menit -› juu fun.C. 12 menit -› juu ni fun.

Dalam bahasa Jepang, penulisan 10 menit adalah 10分 (ぷん) (juppun), sedangkan 12 menit artinya 12分 (ぷん) (ni juu pun).

13. ubah lah dri kalimat aktif ke kalimat pasif 1. The manager will suspend their salary. 2. Who killed the doctor? 3. Some teachers proposed him headmaster.​


ACTIVE-PASSIVE VOICE

1) Their salary will be suspended by the manager.

2) By whom was the doctor killed?

3) He was proposed headmaster by some teachers.

EXPLANATION

Passive voice is commonly used to emphasize the recipient of the verb.

Here are steps how to change the active voice to the passive voice :

~ pay attention to the verb whether it's an intransitive or not. It's commonly we can't change an intransitive verb to the passive voice.

~ the object of an active voice is moved to the subject of a passive voice or instead.

~ all of verbs are conjugated to the past participle verb.

BASED ON YOUR QUESTIONS

1) SIMPLE FUTURE

A : S + will + verb¹+ object.

P : S(O) + will+be + verb³+ by+Agent.

Subject (the manager)+verb (will suspend)+Object (their salary).

changes to :

New subject(their salary)+verb(will be suspended)+by+Agent (the manager).

2) PAST SIMPLE [WH. QUESTION]

A : Who + verb² + object + ?

P : By whom + be verb + object of active + verb³+ ?

WH. word (Who)+verb (killed)+Object (the doctor)?

changes to :

By whom+ was (be verb) + the doctor (object of active)+ killed (3rd verb) ?

"Who" is used as a subject. But, because it is an interrogative sentence, we have to use the be-verb "Was" before the passive subject. The use of Be verb at the beginning is as a feature of a yes or no question. A WH-question must start with a WH word.

3) PAST SIMPLE (AFFIRMATIVE)

A : S + verb² + O

P : S(O) + BE (was/were) + verb³+by Agent.

Subject (some teachers)+verb(proposed)+Indirect Object(him)+ direct object (headmaster)

changes to :

New subject (he)+ verb (was proposed) + Direct object (headmaster) by + Agent (some teachers)

Konversi heksadesimal (bilangan berbasis 16) ke biner (bilangan berbasis 2) dapat dilakukan dg bantuan tabel sistem bilangan. Caranya:

masing-masing digit desimal dituliskan jadi biner dalam format 4 digit. Sehingga:

D (16) = 1101 (2)

2 (16) = 0010 (2)

Jadi D2 (16) = 11010010 (2)

5 (16) = 0101 (2)

C (16) = 1100 (2)

Jadi 5C (16) = 1011100 (2)

Amatilah tabel sistem bilangan di lampiran.

18. Change the following sentences into possive voice 1. The man gave her an expensive farfume 2. The headmaster is going to suspend any student addicted the drugs 3. We have decorated the room for the party


Answer :

1. The man is given her an expensive parfume (Present Simple Tense)

2. The headmaster is being gone to suspend any student addicted the drugs (Present Continuous Tense)

3. We have been done decorated the room for party (Present Perfect Tense)

semoga terbantu:)

PASSIVE VOICE

1) She was given an expensive parfume [by the man]

2) Any student addicted the drugs is going to be suspended [by the headmaster]

3) The room has been decorated for the party [by us]

EXPLANATION

Active voice adalah gramatikal voice, di mana subyek melakukan aksinya terhadap obyek.

Passive voice adalah gramatikal voice, di mana subyek dikenai tindakan oleh si obyek.

Rumus identifikasi utama dari passive voice yaitu Subject+Aux. Be+ Verb³.

Berdasarkan soal yg ditanya :

1) Simple Past

Active ➧ S+verb²+Object......

The man gave her an expensive farfume

Passive ➧ S+was/were+verb3+by.....

She was given an expensive parfume [by the man]

or

An expensive parfume was given to her [by the man]

2) Simple Future "Be going to"

Active ➧ S+is/am/are+going to+verb1+O

The headmaster is going to suspend any student addicted the drugs.

Passive ➧ S+is/am/are+going+to+be+verb³+by..

Any student addicted the drugs is going to be suspended [by the headmaster]

3) Present Perfect

Active ➧ S+has/have+verb3+O

We have decorated the room for the party

Passive ➧ S+has/have+been+verb3+by Agent..

The room has been decorated for the party [by us]
