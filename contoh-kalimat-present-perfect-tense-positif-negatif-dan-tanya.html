1. Contoh kalimat present perfect continuous tense positif negatif dan tanya


Jawaban:

(+) she have been sleeping for 2 hours.

(-) she haven't been sleeping for 2 hours.

(?) have she been sleeping for 2 hours?

Present perfect tense dipakai untuk menyatakan perbuatan atau situasi yang dimulai di masa lampau dan sudah selesai atau masih terjadi atau masih ada hubungannya dengan masa sekarang.

Formula untuk positive:

Subject + has/have + Verb 3

Formula untuk negative:

Subject + has/have (not) + Verb 3

Formula untuk interrogative:

Has/have + subject + Verb 3 (?)

Has: She, he, it

Have: I, you, they, we

Contoh kalimat:

(+) I have eaten breakfast.

(-) I have not eaten breakfast.

(?) Have I eaten breakfast?

(+) She has done her homework.

(-) She has not done her homework.

(?) Has she done her homework?

4. Buatkan 12 kalimat positif, negatif, dan interogatif present perfect tense beserta rumus present perfect tense!​


PRESENT PERFECT TENSE

Rumus untuk kalimat verbal :

(+) S + have / has + verb 3 (past participle)

(-) S + have / has + not + verb 3 (past participle)

(?) Have / has  + S + verb 3 (past participle)

Contoh kalimat verbal :

(+) Father has fixed the broken window

(-) She hasn't recognized the man at first

(?) Have you met her yesterday?

(+) They have completed the task earlier

(-) They haven't built the tree house yet

(?) Has he told you that we have an exam today?

Rumus untuk kalimat nominal :

(+) S + has / have + been + non verb + O

(-) S + has / have + not + been +  non verb + O

(?) Has / have + S + been +  non verb + O

Contoh kalimat nominal :

(+) They have been best friends since they were kid

(-) He hasn't been sick yesterday. So we made an appointment to meet

(?) Has he ever been to abroad before?

(+) There have been many ups and downs in their marriages

(-) It has not been another excellent year for her

(?) Have you been busy for these two weeks? Because you never phoned me

Catatan :

Have digunakan untuk kata ganti orang pertama (I / you / we / they) dan subjek jamak. Has digunakan untuk orang ketiga tunggal (she / he) dan subjek tunggal (it)

18. tuliskan 5 contoh present perfect tense dan buat kalimat positif negatif dan introgatif (+-?)


Berikut 5 contoh present perfect tense :

1. (+) My Parents have lived in Malang.

(-) My Parents have not lived in Malang.

(?) Have My Parent lived in Malang?

2. (+) Vina has been at class since six o'clock.

(-) Vina has not been at class since six o'clock.

(?) Has Vina been at class since six o'clock?

3. (+) They have known each other since they were child.

(-) They have not known each other since they were child.

(?) Have they known each other since they were child?

4. (+) He has been a teacher for five years.

(-) He has not been a teacher for five years.

(?) Has he been a teacher for five years?

5. (+) You have been here for three months.

(-) You have not been here for three months.

(?) Have you been here for three months.

Pembahasan :

Simple Present Perfect Tense merupakan tenses yang digunakan untuk menunjukkan aktivitas yang terjadi di masa lampau dan masih berdampak sampai sekarang. Pada tenses ini biasanya ditandai dengan since atau for, perbedaan keduanya adalah :

Since digunakan jika menunjukkan waktu tertentu (a particular time).For digunakan jika menunjukkan durasi atau lamanya waktu (a duration of time).

rumus :

VERBAL, jika predikat berupa verb,

(+) Subject + Have/has + V3 + Object.

(-) Subject + Have/has + not + V3 + Object.

(?) Have/has + Subject + V3 + Object?

NON-VERBAL/NOMINAL, jika predikat berupa been,

(+) Subject + Have/has + been + Complement.

(-) Subject + Have/has + not + been + Complement.

(?) Have/has + Subject + been + Complement?

contoh :

VERBAL,

My Parents have lived in Malang.They have not known each other since they were child.Have you studied?

NON-VERBAL/NOMINAL,

Vina has been at class since six o'clock.He has not been a teacher for five years.Have you been here for three months?

keterangan :

Have hanya digunakan jika subjek berupa I, we, you, they, it (plural).Has hanya digunakan jika subjek berupa she, he, it (singular).
