14. tolong ya master master pro​


Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

(3x - 1) (x + 2) - 3 (x + 1) = 0

3x² + 6x - x - 2 - 3x - 3 = 0

3x² + 2x - 5 = 0

(3x + 5) . (x - 1) =0

x - 1 = 0

x = 1

3x + 5 = 0

3x = -5

x = -5/3

Hasil akar-akar

= 1 . (-5/3)

= -5/3

Kode Kategorisasi : 11.2.5

Kelas 11

Pelajaran 2 - Matematika

Bab 5 - Matriks

[tex]\left|\begin{array}{cc}3x-1 & 3 \\ x+1 & x+2 \end{array}\right| = 0 \\ [/tex]

Hasil kali akar-akar persamaannya = ...?

Penyelesaian:

[tex]\left|\begin{array}{cc}3x-1 & 3 \\ x+1 & x+2 \end{array}\right| = 0 \\ (3x-1)(x+2)-3(x+1) = 0 \\ 3x^2+5x-2-3x-3 = 0\\3x^2+2x-5 =0\\[/tex]

Dari [tex]3x^2+2x-5 = 0[/tex] diperoleh

a = 3

b = 2

c = -5

Nilai hasil kali akar-akar persamaan kuadrat:

[tex]\begin{aligned} x_1\times x_2 &= \frac{c}{a} \\ &= \frac{-5}{3} \\ &= -\frac{5}{3}\end{aligned}[/tex]

17. skipper means captain​


Jawaban:

The word “skipper” originated from the Dutch word schipper, which means “a person who commands a ship” . A schipper leads his crew to their destination, or achieve some other goal. In sport, the captain of a team has the same job to fulfill. Hence, he is called “skipper”

Jawaban:

General Knowledge

A skipper is known as a captain of a boat or a ship.
