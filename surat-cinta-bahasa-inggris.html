17. contoh surat untuk ayah/ibu tercinta dalam bahasa Inggris​


Jawaban:

LOVE LETTERS FOR PARENTS

Penjelasan:

For dear Mama and Papa......

Mama, thank you for bringing me into this world. Thank you for letting me know how the world lives. Without you I would not be in this world. Your love and sincerity of your love are like flowing water that never stops coming to me every second. You are willing to risk your life to give birth to me. You are an angel of the world who always takes care of me, the teacher in my life who teaches me many things all the time. You are an angel sent by Allah SWT to me. When I was your little fairy, you always took care of me and took care of me without complaining. Even though your body feels tired, a glimmer of your smile still shines through your days when you see your little fairy. now, the little fairy is starting to grow up. Mama, I am very grateful to you. You have educated me until I become a good person. I don't waste your sacrifice. I'm sorry I can't repay your services. Until I sacrifice my life, I will not be able to repay all your sacrifices. Every drop of your sweat is like a pearl that nothing can pay for.

Papa, thank you for educating me. You are my hero who always protects me from all dangers. From morning to night you work to meet our needs. Even though the sweat was pouring from your forehead, I never even heard your complaints. Sometimes you educate me in ways that I don't understand, but now that I am getting older, I slowly understand that you don't want me to fall into free association. I can get an education thanks to your hard work. I do not know how I repay your services and sacrifices. I know, your prayers are always there for me in every prayer.

I know not to be afraid, your hands are always for me to hold mine. I know if I run too fast, you will follow me to help me get up if I trip and fall. Your love has never diminished, even when now I am standing on my own feet.

I'm sorry, when I was growing up I rarely said that "I Love You". But believe me, from the bottom of my heart "I really love you more than myself", never even less.

Thanks for you I can exist in this world. Thank you for taking care of me from childhood until I grew up the way I am today. You are the best parents. You are the most beautiful gift from Allah to me.

Greetings Dear Always,

From your beloved child
